# Brand Strategy

## Brand Identity
**Parent Brand:** Neil Greene
**Positioning Title:** Architect of Power & Discipline
**Coaching Brand:** Forged By War

## Core Mission
Forged By War helps men rebuild discipline, strength, clarity, leadership, and direction through structured coaching, accountability, and brotherhood.

## Primary Audience
Men ages 35 to 55 who are high-responsibility, ambitious, and financially stable, generally earning $150,000+ annually.

They are often:
- successful on paper but drifting physically, mentally, or spiritually
- carrying pressure at work and at home
- frustrated by inconsistency, lack of structure, or loss of edge
- looking for accountability, discipline, brotherhood, and measurable progress

## Secondary Audience
Corporate event planners, leadership teams, organizations, and conference hosts looking for a speaker on discipline, leadership, resilience, performance, and personal responsibility.

## Business Goals
The website should support these goals:
1. Generate qualified leads for 1-on-1 coaching
2. Fill quarterly group coaching cohorts
3. Grow the Forged By War Skool community
4. Generate speaking inquiries
5. Use the book *Ignite: Fitness Fuel for Working Dads* as an authority and trust-building asset
6. Capture leads through strategic lead magnets

## Brand Promise
This brand is not generic motivation. It is a structured path for men who are done drifting and ready to rebuild power, discipline, and direction.

## Brand Characteristics
- masculine
- premium
- disciplined
- direct
- calm
- credible
- structured
- selective
- transformational

## What the Brand Must Avoid
- generic life-coach language
- cheesy motivation
- fake alpha language
- overhyped promises
- overly soft wellness language
- manipulative scarcity
- cluttered design
- broad messaging that tries to serve everyone

## Core Conversion Paths
1. Visitor → Private Coaching Application
2. Visitor → Group Coaching Cohort Application
3. Visitor → Skool Community
4. Visitor → Speaking Inquiry
5. Visitor → Lead Magnet → Email List → Offer Path
6. Visitor → Book → Community or Coaching
