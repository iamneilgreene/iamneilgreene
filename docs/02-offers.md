# Offers

## Offer Ladder

### 1. Forged By War Brotherhood
**Type:** Community / Skool Membership
**Role:** Entry-level ecosystem and ongoing brotherhood

**Purpose:**
- give men a lower-friction way to enter the brand
- build trust and belonging
- provide accountability and community
- feed higher-tier coaching offers

**Likely Includes:**
- Skool community access
- group accountability
- live Q&A or monthly call
- challenges, prompts, or frameworks
- shared brotherhood environment

---

### 2. Forged By War Cohort
**Type:** Quarterly Group Coaching
**Role:** Mid-tier structured transformation offer

**Purpose:**
- provide a more guided and outcome-driven coaching experience
- create stronger accountability and momentum
- offer a premium but more scalable option than 1-on-1

**Recommended Structure:**
- 12-week cohort
- weekly group coaching
- accountability checkpoints
- limited seats
- community access included

---

### 3. Forged By War Private
**Type:** 1-on-1 Coaching
**Role:** Flagship premium offer

**Purpose:**
- serve serious men who want private, direct coaching
- provide personalized structure, discipline, and accountability
- create the highest-status transformation offer

**Recommended Structure:**
- 12-week minimum engagement
- weekly private coaching call
- direct accountability
- personalized training / discipline / performance support
- application only

---
